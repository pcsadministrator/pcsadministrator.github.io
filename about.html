About Platinum Code

Platinum Code is a web site and software design company based at the foothills of the Rocky Mountains in Longmont, Colorado. We are well poised to meet all of your information technology needs. Our particular strengths include professional web application development and web site design, software design and development, and interactive multimedia solutions to small and medium sized businesses interested in outsourcing their IT requirements to the most reliable and innovative company at an affordable cost. Our expertise includes software solutions, ranging from the development of middleware and database back-end solutions to powerful user application software, and includes internet solutions, from original and professional looking web sites to powerful content management systems and complete database driven web applications for e-commerce shopping carts and other business logic.
