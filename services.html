Services

Platinum Code is your experienced and dependable Information Technology partner. We will listen to your business requirements and translate them into a robust business solution built on a rock-solid architecture that meets all your requirements while remaining efficient, reliable, and cost effective. Platinum Code provides a warranty on all work so as to insure your satisfaction. You will find that our rates are quite reasonable, and our service and support to be exceptional. Please contact us now to obtain an estimate for your next development project. Platinum Code specializes in C++, Java, PHP, Javascript, and C# solutions running on Windows or Linux/UNIX systems. , and LAMP (Linux, Apache, MySQL, & PHP) solutions.

Services include:

  • Software consulting services: We will analyze your business model and deliver knowledgeable advice and solid solutions
  • Desktop application development: We build user-friendly desktop solutions using C++, C#, or Java
  • Server application development: Using SOAP or REST technology, we implement server applications using Java servlets, PHP, or C++
  • Web application development: Centralize your business application logic on the web for benefits including ease of updates and maintenance, and social networking features
  • Enterprise application integration: Connect your web site or other applications to your back end CRM & ERP business systems and external systems such as Facebook, Salesforce.com, SugarCRM, etc.
  • Data warehousing: Consolidate all of your business intelligence in one place for easy querying and reporting
  • Content management systems: We prefer to use the Drupal CMS atop the LAMP or WAMP platform
  • Middleware messaging systems: We can connect your disparate computing systems with an Enterprise Service Bus and get them talking
  • Web site design: Our expert design staff can give your site a facelift or create an original design
  • Operations, maintenance and support consulting: We'll analyze your infrastructure and keep things running smoothly
  • And more: Don't see what you need? Just ask! Chances are, we can make it happen!
